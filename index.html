About Us!

We were founded by a dude who justed wanted to bring old-school, Bronx-style pizza to LA. You can decide if we achieved our goal.

We've been here in Santa Monica for 25 years, and all that time, we've been selling quality pizza at affordable prices. Whether you want a single slice or a whole pie, we'll be sure to get what you need.

Not to mention, we've been a proud part of the local community serving students and commuters alike. Pretty sure 90% of Santa Monica College students get their lunch here!

So, just hop on down to Pico Blvd and check us out! And, make sure to say hi to our renown Pizza Chef Thomas.
